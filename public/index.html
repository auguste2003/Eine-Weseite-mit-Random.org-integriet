The Wide Range of Offerings Will Fascinate You

Coin Flip: In a coin toss, there are two possible outcomes: heads or tails. The player selects either heads or tails, and then a coin is tossed.

Keno: Keno is a type of lottery game where players try to match the selected numbers with the drawn numbers. The player selects a specific number of numbers from a predefined pool of numbers. Then, random numbers are drawn.
Lotto Quick Pick: Similar to Keno, the goal is to predict the drawn numbers. However, unlike Keno, the numbers are often selected from a smaller pool. The player can either choose their own numbers or use the "Quick Pick" option, where the system automatically generates a random combination of numbers. Then, the draw takes place, and winnings are awarded based on matches.

Lotto Quick Pick. : Take a Look!

Using Quick Pick is intended to ensure that every player has an equal chance, regardless of the selected numbers. Players who don't have specific numbers in mind or simply have no preferences often use the Quick Pick feature for convenience.
